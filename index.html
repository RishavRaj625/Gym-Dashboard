Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We're open Monday to Friday from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and weekends from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM. VIP members have 24/7 access.

Do you offer trial sessions?

Yes! We offer a free 7-day trial for new members. This includes access to all basic facilities and one complimentary personal training session.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons or extended travel. A small administrative fee may apply.

What equipment do you have?

We have state-of-the-art cardio machines, free weights, resistance machines, functional training equipment, and dedicated spaces for yoga and group classes.

Do you provide nutrition guidance?

Yes, our Premium and VIP members receive personalized nutrition guidance from certified nutritionists to complement their fitness goals.
